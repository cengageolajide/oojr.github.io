Good apps should have...

Speed

No one likes using an app that is slow, so speed is essential to the user having a nice experience. Speed is always in fashion. Building apps with a modular approach is becoming the norm. I try to avoid code bloat as much as I can.

Design

I am apart of the camp who think Design is just as important as the engineering. Living in a city like Boston it is very easy to become inspired by design since examples of good design is around every corner. I enjoy working on the front-end of an app more than the back-end.

Content

Content is King is a term that might be a little overused in the web community. Nevertheless, content is very important and if the content is not good, it will be hard for other factors to pick up the slack

My First Web Project

Back in 2011, on the 22nd floor of a UMass dorm tower I made my first site zapslide. The wifi was horrible especially during the weekends and this was my first site but I still got the job done somehow. It doesnt fall under any particular category you can say it is a social network. The freedom I have with this project lets me experiment with different design choices which is fun. I plan to blog more on it, along with a few of my friends and throw in some more interactive projects in there. I like the natural direction it's going in.
